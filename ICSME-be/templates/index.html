How to interpret the diff symbols:

+: Indicates lines that have been added compared to the previous version of the file.

-: Indicates lines that have been deleted compared to the previous version of the file.

Modified lines:
Modified lines are represented by a combination of removed (-) and added (+) lines. In other words, a modified line will appear as a deleted line (preceded by -) and a new added line (preceded by +) in the diff.
